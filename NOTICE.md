# Notice

`cross-treasury` is an AnarchI Technologies CROSS Mainnet skill released under MIT.

It is not an official `to-nexus` package unless and until the upstream team adopts it. It is designed as a free public orchestration layer that composes existing CROSS skills instead of duplicating their protocol drivers.

Brand line: Hardcoding freedom into the systems of tomorrow.

Primary composition targets:

- `cross-stake` for native CROSS staking.
- `cross-forge` for Forge token inspection and trade flows.
- `cross-dex-trade` for GameToken AMM swaps and LP flows.
- `cross-rewards` for rewards-pool staking where applicable.

No secrets are committed, printed, or required for read-only planning.
